Intrepid Club

At Intrepid Club we believe that everyone has a chance to have a unique adventure exprience; we are inclusive, from the youngest ones to the eldest.

Our experienced staff professionally handles all details for you. You'll feel safe and return home with memories to share for a lifetime.
