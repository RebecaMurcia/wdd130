Oregon River Experiences

Clackamas River

The Clackamas River rafting is Portland's most accessible white water river adventure and a great way to experience the thrilling outdoors of Oregon. Being nestled under Mt Hood & Mt Jefferson the river courses its way down the cascades to drain into the Willamette River, just downstream from the thundering Willamette Falls.

Deschutes River

Oregon’s most popular day trip white water river and with good reason. Your Deschutes River rafting trip is full of sun, big splashy waves and boiling rapids. Nestled in a desert river canyon, near the town of Maupin; the Deschutes River not only provides some thrilling rapids like Wapinitia, Boxcar, Surf City and Oak Springs.

McKenzie River

The McKenzie River is an Oregon classic for rafters and for good reason. With crystal clear water swiftly moving by a tree lined river bank full of fun rapids the excitement never stops on this scenic rafting trip. You can choose between two different sections, the upper and lower which both offer mild but consistent rapids, great for family trips.

North Santiam River

With its convenient location near Salem and Portland the North Santiam river rafting trip offers great introductory whitewater. With clear water during the summertime there is lots of swimming chances and moments for relaxation. The mild rapids are still splashy and exciting enough with Spencer's Hole, Carnivore and Mill City Falls, its a river rafting trip suitable for everyone.

North Umpqua River

Tucked near the base of Crater Lake, the North Umpqua River certainly has a lot to offer when it comes to whitewater action. With multiple options for a variety of runs, from consistent class II+ rapids to spaced out bigger class III+ rapids, we think the North Umpqua river is some of the finest whitewater in the state.
